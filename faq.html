Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FLISS Concierge FAQ. Here are the answers to some of the most common questions regarding our luxury fragrance collections.

1. How long do FLISS fragrances last?

Our perfumes are reformulator-crafted at an exceptional 50% oil concentration (reformulated specifically for Indian tropical and humid weather). This premium concentration provides a rich, long-lasting scent trail that lingers for 8 to 12 hours on the skin and even longer on clothing.

2. Are FLISS perfumes unisex?

Yes. We believe that fine fragrances transcend gender boundaries. Our scent profiles (White Light, Citrus Elixir, Woody Oud) are masterfully blended as gender-neutral profiles that develop uniquely on everyone's skin chemistry.

3. Where are your essential oils and ingredients sourced?

We source our premium essential oils and raw ingredients globally from the world's most renowned natural origins. These natural extracts are blended with precision to offer high-concentration premium oils without compromising on skin-safety standards.

4. How do I place an enquiry or complete a purchase?

We operate on an exclusive luxury WhatsApp purchase flow. You can explore our collection on the website, open any fragrance modal, and click "Enquire via WhatsApp". This directly connects you with our brand concierge, who will share prices, process payment details, and confirm shipment.

5. Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer **Free Express Shipping** pan-India for all orders. Additionally, we provide complimentary fragrance samples with every single order so you can experience other scent profiles risk-free.

6. What should I do if my package arrives damaged?

In the rare case of transit damage (e.g. leaking bottle or broken atomizer pump), please contact us via WhatsApp at **+91 94449 26620** within **24 hours of delivery** with photos or a video showing the damage. We will dispatch a brand new replacement bottle to you within 1-2 business days at no extra cost.
