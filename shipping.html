Shipping Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

At FLISS, we aim to deliver our luxury fragrances to you in the safest, most efficient manner. Please review our shipping practices below.

1. Free Shipping Pan-India

We are delighted to offer **Complimentary Free Shipping** across India for all perfume purchases. No minimum cart value or discount code is required.

2. Order Processing & Dispatch

Every FLISS fragrance is packaged with extreme care. Orders are typically processed and dispatched within **1 to 2 business days** of enquiry confirmation.

Orders are not processed or shipped on Sundays and national holidays.

3. Delivery Timelines

Once dispatched, estimated delivery timelines are as follows:

  • Metro Cities: 2 to 4 business days.
  • Rest of India: 4 to 7 business days.

Note: Deliveries may occasionally experience minor delays due to extreme weather condition, public holidays, or courier service exceptions. We work with premium domestic express couriers to minimize delays.

4. Shipment Tracking

Once your order is handed over to our courier partner, we will share the tracking number and a tracking link directly with you via WhatsApp or Email. This allows you to monitor the journey of your package in real-time.

5. Damaged Shipments

Our packaging is custom-crafted to be highly impact-resistant. However, in the rare event that your perfume bottle arrives damaged or broken during transit, please take a photo or video of the packaging and product immediately and contact us via WhatsApp within **24 hours of delivery** so we can arrange a replacement.
