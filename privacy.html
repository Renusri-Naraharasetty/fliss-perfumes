Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

Welcome to FLISS Perfumes. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and submit reviews or enquiries.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you directly provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Review Form Submissions: Full Name, Email Address, Star Rating, and Review Message.
  • Customer Enquiries: Information shared when communicating via our WhatsApp buttons or contact links.
  • Usage Data: Basic technical diagnostics, such as your IP address, browser type, and page interactions to help improve website performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Instantly publish customer reviews and testimonials on the public website.
  • Prevent spam, validate reviewer emails, and manage submission cooldowns.
  • Respond to WhatsApp enquiries and process orders efficiently.
  • Maintain website stability and analyze overall platform usage.

3. Third-Party Integrations

We utilize trusted third-party services to deliver a premium user experience:

  • Firebase Firestore: Used for secure storage of submitted customer reviews.
  • WhatsApp: Direct links connect you to our brand agents for support and sales.
  • Google Fonts & GSAP: Loaded externally to maintain typography and smooth editorial animations.

4. Security & Data Protection

We implement secure data practices to prevent unauthorized access or alteration of your reviews. Email addresses collected through the review form are used solely for verification and spam reduction, and are never shared or sold to external third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the modification or deletion of your review at any time. To request removal of your published testimonial, please contact us at flissperfumes@gmail.com.
