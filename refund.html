Refund Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

Thank you for choosing FLISS Perfumes. Because of the personal care and luxury nature of our high-concentration fragrances, we maintain a strict returns and refunds policy to ensure hygiene and product integrity.

1. Policy on General Returns

We **do not accept returns, exchanges, or refunds** for opened, sprayed, or used perfume products if you simply change your mind, do not prefer the scent profile, or purchased the incorrect variant by mistake.

To assist in your discovery journey, we offer complimentary samples with every order so you can experience other fragrance profiles risk-free before ordering them.

2. Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Deliveries

We will gladly arrange a **Free Replacement** if you receive a product that is:

  • Broken or leaking during transit.
  • Defective (e.g. broken atomizer spray pump).
  • Incorrect (e.g. you received Woody Oud instead of Citrus Elixir).

3. Filing a Claim

To request a replacement for a damaged or incorrect shipment:

  • Submit a claim within **24 hours of receiving the delivery**. Claims made after 24 hours cannot be verified and may not be processed.
  • Please send an email to flissperfumes@gmail.com or message us directly on WhatsApp at **+91 94449 26620**.
  • Your request must include: your order name/details, delivery address, and clear photo or video proof of the damaged or incorrect bottle and packaging.

4. Replacement Fulfillment

Once your claim is validated by our customer care team, we will dispatch a brand new replacement bottle immediately within **1 to 2 business days** at zero additional shipping cost to you. Tracking details for the replacement will be sent directly via WhatsApp.
